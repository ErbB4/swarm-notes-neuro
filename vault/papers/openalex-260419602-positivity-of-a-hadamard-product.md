# Positivity of a Hadamard Product

**Authors**: Roger A. Horn, Shengxuan Luo, Hongwei Xu, Zai Yang
**Date**: 2026-04-21
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2604.19602](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19602)

## Summary

This paper investigates the positivity properties of the Hadamard product, focusing on the counterintuitive case where the Hadamard product of singular positive semidefinite matrices can yield a nonsingular result. The authors derive a new eigenvalue lower bound for the Hadamard product that incorporates the rank, effective condition number, and diagonal entries of one factor alongside the smallest eigenvalues of principal submatrices of the other. Theoretical results are supplemented by numerical examples, with specific applications proposed for array signal processing and matrix time series analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Establishes a novel eigenvalue lower bound for the Hadamard product of two matrices.
- Demonstrates that the Hadamard product of singular positive semidefinite matrices can result in a nonsingular matrix.
- Provides applications of the bound in array signal processing and matrix time series analysis.
