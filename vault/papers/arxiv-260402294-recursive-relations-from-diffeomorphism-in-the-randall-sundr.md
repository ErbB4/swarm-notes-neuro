# Recursive relations from diffeomorphism in the Randall-Sundrum model

**Authors**: Haiying Cai, Giacomo Cacciapaglia
**Date**: 2026-04-02
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2604.02294](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02294)

## Summary

This paper investigates the implications of diffeomorphism invariance in warped extra-dimensional gravity models, specifically the Randall-Sundrum scenario. By deriving nonlinear transformation rules for metric perturbations within the unitary gauge, the authors identify recursive relations that govern the field expansion of the effective Lagrangian. These off-shell symmetries provide a structured approach to analyzing the expansion orders of the theory.

## Key Contributions

- Derivation of nonlinear diffeomorphism transformation rules for metric perturbations in the unitary gauge.
- Establishment of recursive relations between consecutive orders in the effective Lagrangian field expansion.
- Demonstration of the off-shell nature of diffeomorphism symmetry in warped extra-dimension gravity models.
