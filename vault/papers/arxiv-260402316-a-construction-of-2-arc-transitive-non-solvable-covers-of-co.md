# A construction of 2-arc-transitive non-solvable covers of complete graphs

**Authors**: Jiyong Chen, Cai Heng Li, Ci Xuan Wu, Yan Zhou Zhu
**Date**: 2026-04-02
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2604.02316](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02316)

## Summary

This paper addresses the classification of symmetric graphs by constructing connected 2-arc-transitive covers of complete graphs where the transformation group is a non-abelian characteristically simple group. This construction effectively resolves the existence problem regarding non-solvable 2-arc-transitive covers of complete graphs. The findings contribute to the broader structural theory of 2-arc-transitive graphs and their automorphism groups.

## Key Contributions

- Constructs connected 2-arc-transitive covers of complete graphs with non-abelian characteristically simple transformation groups.
- Resolves the long-standing existence problem for non-solvable 2-arc-transitive covers of complete graphs.
