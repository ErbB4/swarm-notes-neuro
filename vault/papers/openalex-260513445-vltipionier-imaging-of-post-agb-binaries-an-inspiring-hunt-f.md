# VLTI/PIONIER imaging of post-AGB binaries. An INSPIRING hunt for inner rim substructures in circumbinary discs

**Authors**: Toon De Prins, Akke Corporaal, J. Kluska, D. Kamath, Hans Van Winckel, Kateryna Andrych, Javier Alcolea, Narsireddy Anugu, Jean-Philippe Berger, V. Bujarrabal, I. Gallardo Cava, Stefan Kraus, Hans Olofsson
**Date**: 2026-05-13
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2605.13445](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13445)

## Summary

This paper provides a detailed interferometric imaging survey of the inner rim morphology of eight post-AGB circumbinary discs using VLTI/PIONIER data. By applying a specialized image reconstruction workflow integrating the SPARCO approach and the ORGANIC algorithm, the study achieves resolutions of 1-2 mas. The results reveal diverse, dynamic inner disc regions with frequent non-axisymmetric substructures, suggesting significant dynamical or radiative responses to central binaries or hydrodynamical instabilities.

## Key Contributions

- Presents the first homogeneous interferometric imaging survey of inner post-AGB circumbinary disc regions at 1-2 mas resolution.
- Develops and applies a robust image reconstruction workflow combining the SPARCO approach with the ORGANIC algorithm for VLTI/PIONIER data.
- Identifies robust azimuthal brightness enhancements and complex morphological features in a majority of sampled targets that cannot be explained by simple inclination effects.
