# Asymptotic behavior of spatio-temporal point processes of exceedances

**Authors**: Carolin Forster, Marco Oesting
**Date**: 2026-04-13
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2604.11691](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11691)

## Summary

This paper provides a theoretical analysis of the asymptotic behavior of spatio-temporal point processes of exceedances, where points are defined by rescaled occurrence times, locations, and exceedance values. By utilizing the framework of stationary regularly varying multivariate time series, the authors demonstrate the weak convergence of these point processes. The study explicitly derives the limit distribution for exceedances, allowing for flexible thresholding via site-dependent risk functionals.

## Key Contributions

- Established the theoretical framework for the weak convergence of spatio-temporal point processes of exceedances.
- Derived the limit distribution for exceedances defined by site-dependent risk functionals in stationary regularly varying multivariate time series.
- Unified and extended existing asymptotic results for extreme value theory within a spatio-temporal context.
