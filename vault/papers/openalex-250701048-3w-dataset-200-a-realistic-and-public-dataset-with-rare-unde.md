## Summary

The 3W Dataset 2.0.0 is an updated, expert-labeled multivariate time series benchmark designed for detecting rare undesirable events in oil wells. This version expands upon the original 2019 release by incorporating more instances, additional monitored variables, and a refined data structure for improved accessibility. It serves as a critical resource for developing predictive maintenance methodologies capable of enabling early mitigating actions in the oil industry.

## Key Contributions

- Presents the updated version 2.0.0 of the 3W Dataset, featuring an increased number of instances and monitored variables.
- Introduces a new labeling category and a refined data structure for improved efficiency and robustness in accessing multivariate oil well time series.
- Provides a foundational benchmark for detecting and anticipating rare undesirable events in industrial oil production environments.
