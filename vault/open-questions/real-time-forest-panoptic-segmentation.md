title: Real-time Forest Panoptic Segmentation
---

**Background:** Panoptic segmentation in 3D point clouds requires robust classification of hierarchical structures across varying environmental conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending panoptic segmentation models from offline, batch-processed point cloud analysis to real-time, single-scan on-board processing integrated within a robotic mapping framework remains an unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Real-time on-board semantic scene understanding is a critical bottleneck for fully autonomous forestry operations, as it enables robots to make informed, immediate decisions based on the structural properties of the forest environment.
