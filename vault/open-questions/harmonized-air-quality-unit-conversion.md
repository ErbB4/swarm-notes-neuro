title: Harmonization of Monitoring Units
---

**Background:** AirQualityBench preserves provider-native unit metadata, but this leads to heterogeneity in concentration scales across monitoring sources, particularly for gaseous pollutants. Providing a fully harmonized dataset with station-level unit audits and explicit conversion rules remains an unresolved task.

**Question / Future Work:** The challenge lies in creating a fully harmonized version of the benchmark, complete with station-level unit audits and validated conversion rules, to ensure robust scientific analysis across different geographic regions and monitoring providers.

**Why It Matters:** Critical for ensuring researchers can reliably compare forecasting performance across different geographic regions and monitoring providers without being confounded by unit mismatches.

**Evidence:** A fully harmonized release with station-level unit audits and explicit conversion rules is an important future extension.
