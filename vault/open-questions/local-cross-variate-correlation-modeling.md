title: Modeling Local Cross-Variate Correlations
---

**Background:** Time series forecasting models often struggle to effectively capture inter-variate correlations at a local, fine-grained temporal resolution.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop methods that explicitly incorporate local-level cross-variate correlations within hybrid architectures, as current approaches often rely on global context to capture such interactions, potentially missing relevant local dependencies.

**Why It Matters:** This represents a significant architectural bottleneck in multivariate time series forecasting that prevents models from capturing the complex, localized interaction patterns between different variables.
