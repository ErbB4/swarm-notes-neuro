title: Causes of Extreme Forecast Errors
---

**Background:** Mixture-of-Experts architectures in time series forecasting can experience performance degradation when evaluated with certain error metrics that are sensitive to large deviations. This suggests a tendency to produce extreme forecast values in specific scenarios.

**Question / Future Work:** The underlying causes of extreme predictions in MoE time series forecasting models remain unresolved. Further work is needed to diagnose why these models occasionally generate large forecast errors and to develop mitigation strategies for these outliers.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding and mitigating extreme prediction errors is crucial for the reliability and risk management applications of time series forecasting models.
