title: Event-specific covariate selection for risk
---

**Background:** Operational risk categories exhibit varying levels of sensitivity to macroeconomic indicators, necessitating the identification of appropriate exogenous covariates for different event types.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should explore the inclusion of alternative, event-specific macroeconomic or environmental indicators—such as interest rates, climate-related metrics, or geopolitical risk indices—to better explain and predict variations in operational losses for categories where standard indices are less effective.

**Why It Matters:** Identifying the correct covariates for different risk categories is essential for accurate stress testing and capital planning in financial institutions.
