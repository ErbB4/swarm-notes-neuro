title: Adaptive shape index for RV-DLMs
---

**Background:** Bayesian dynamic models for financial time series often face challenges in maintaining accurate precision estimates when the quality or microstructure noise of high-frequency realized volatility proxies changes over time.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of adaptive shape index parameters (αt) for dynamic gamma processes in realized volatility modeling is needed to better account for time-varying measurement precision and enhance robustness during regimes of shifting market microstructure noise.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental limitation of static precision modeling in high-frequency financial econometrics, where data quality is inherently non-stationary.
