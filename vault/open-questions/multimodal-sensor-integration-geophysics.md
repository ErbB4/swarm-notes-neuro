title: Multimodal Sensor Integration Generalization
---

**Background:** Atmospheric world models integrate diverse sensor modalities to learn a unified representation of Earth system dynamics. Extending the range of data sources can improve the generalizability and comprehensiveness of these latent representations.

**Question / Future Work:** Future development should focus on integrating a broader spectrum of geophysical sensors and modalities into the latent observation space, enabling a more holistic Earth-system digital twin beyond traditional atmospheric or oceanic variables.

**Why It Matters:** A more diverse data integration enables the model to capture a wider range of physical phenomena and cross-sphere interactions, which is essential for a true digital twin of the Earth system.
