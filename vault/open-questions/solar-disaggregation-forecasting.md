title: Solar PV Load Disaggregation
---

**Background:** Residential energy forecasting often conflates grid consumption and solar generation into a single net signal, complicating model interpretation and performance. Separating these latent drivers is essential for accurate short-term load forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop architectures capable of dual-output forecasting to disentangle load and solar generation signals, preventing static meteorological features from acting as proxies for unobserved generation patterns.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for addressing the performance bias observed when models implicitly learn solar patterns rather than true energy demand.
