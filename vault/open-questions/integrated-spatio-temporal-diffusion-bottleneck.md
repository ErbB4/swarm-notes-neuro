title: Integrated Spatio-Temporal Diffusion Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Graph-based diffusion models for time-series forecasting often address spatial and temporal dependencies separately or through heuristic coupling.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a critical need for unified architectures that explicitly integrate spatial graph dependencies with temporal dynamics within a single generative framework, as opposed to relying on decoupled modeling. Investigating this integration is essential for capturing complex, non-stationary dynamic graph signals accurately.

**Why It Matters:** The current decoupling of space and time is a fundamental limitation in generative graph modeling, hindering the capacity to capture high-order dependencies in dynamic signals.
