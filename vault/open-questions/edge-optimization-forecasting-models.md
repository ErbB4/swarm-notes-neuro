title: Edge Optimization for Forecasting Models
---

**Background:** Building energy management systems increasingly require localized, real-time control at the edge. The deployment of high-performance deep learning models on resource-constrained hardware remains a significant bottleneck.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to optimize complex forecasting network architectures, such as the proposed patch-based fusion models, for efficient deployment on edge devices. This includes exploring network compression techniques to maintain predictive performance while reducing computational overhead.

**Why It Matters:** Edge-based forecasting is necessary for local demand-side management and reducing data transmission latencies, but current deep learning models are often too computationally demanding for these environments.
