title: Standardizing Multilingual Variable Names
---

**Background:** Oil and gas production datasets often contain specialized industry terminology, acronyms, and units derived from local languages, which limits global interpretability and accessibility.

**Question / Future Work:** Variable names and metadata in domain-specific time series datasets often rely on local languages (e.g., Portuguese), creating a barrier to international research collaboration and reproducibility. Standardizing these into a universal lexicon is essential for the longevity of the benchmark.

**Why It Matters:** Standardizing terminology in a global research dataset is critical for reproducibility and lowering the barrier to entry for international researchers.

**Evidence:** Note that some variable names contain terms or acronyms in Portuguese... Translating all these names into English is a pending issue that will be resolved in future versions of 3W Dataset.
