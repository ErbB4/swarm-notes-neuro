title: LLM Structural Transparency Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly applied to time series forecasting by transforming numerical data into textual narratives, but the reliance on implicit representations often limits their structural interpretability.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing methodologies that balance LLM reasoning capabilities with explicit structural transparency for high-stakes, co-evolving time series forecasting remains an open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** This bottleneck is critical for the adoption of LLMs in fields requiring auditability and causal understanding of forecasting outputs.
