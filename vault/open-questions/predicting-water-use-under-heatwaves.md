title: Predicting water-use during heatwaves
---

**Background:** Tree water-use behavior significantly shifts during and after extreme weather events like heatwaves, making standard predictive models based on normal environmental conditions less accurate.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust predictive models for tree water-use that account for non-stationary physiological responses induced by extreme climate events, such as heatwaves and sustained drought, remains a significant challenge. This involves identifying model structures or dynamic parameter adjustments that capture abrupt changes in plant water extraction behavior.

**Why It Matters:** As heatwaves increase in frequency and severity, predicting their impact on plant water consumption is essential for sustainable irrigation management in agriculture and forestry.
