title: Multivariate extension for RV-DLMs
---

**Background:** Bayesian dynamic models are frequently restricted to bivariate or low-dimensional settings, limiting their utility for complex portfolio management and large-scale risk assessment where cross-asset dependencies are critical.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending bivariate price-realized volatility models to higher-dimensional multivariate settings—specifically by utilizing Wishart and multivariate-F distributions alongside realized variance-covariance matrices—is necessary for scalable risk management.

**Why It Matters:** Transitioning to multivariate frameworks is essential for applying these dynamic models to realistic financial tasks like risk-parity or large-scale portfolio construction.
