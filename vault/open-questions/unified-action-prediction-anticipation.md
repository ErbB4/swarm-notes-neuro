title: Unified Action Prediction Framework

**Background:** Early action prediction involves forecasting future human activities from incomplete video sequences, a task that currently lacks a unified framework capable of handling both prediction and long-term anticipation.

**Question / Future Work:** The study highlights the need to develop a single, unified methodology capable of addressing both action anticipation and early action prediction, rather than treating them as distinct, disjointed research problems.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging these subfields could lead to more robust and versatile video understanding systems that handle diverse temporal horizons consistently.

**Evidence:** Future research directions include ... finding a unified method for both action anticipation and early action prediction.
