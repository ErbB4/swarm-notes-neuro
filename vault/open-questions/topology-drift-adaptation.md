title: Adaptation to Topology Drift
---

**Background:** Microservice-based systems are subject to structural changes such as call-path switching, service insertion, or removal, which can invalidate models trained on fixed topology assumptions.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop robust methods for online adaptation to topology drift or automated model retraining when significant structural changes occur in the microservice call graph.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical bottleneck for deploying predictive auto-scaling in dynamic, production-grade cloud environments where microservice architectures evolve continuously.
