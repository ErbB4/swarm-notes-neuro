title: 3D Geometric Realignment for Unstructured Navigation
---

**Background:** Current geometric re-projection methods for asynchronous robot control often rely on 2D SE(2) transformations that assume flat, structured surfaces. Extending these geometric compensations to 3D navigation in unstructured or uneven environments remains a challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the extension of current SE(2)-based geometric re-projection mechanisms to full 3D spatial mapping, specifically addressing how to represent and align temporal intent misalignment in unstructured, non-planar 3D environments.

**Why It Matters:** As robotic navigation shifts toward 3D mobility (e.g., legged robots on uneven terrain), current 2D-restricted geometric corrections represent a fundamental bottleneck to robust cloud-edge collaboration.
