title: Weather-informed scenario generation
---

**Background:** Current scenario generation approaches for intraday electricity prices are typically restricted to available historical and fundamental data sources, often omitting richer external information such as meteorological ensemble forecasts.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the integration of external meteorological ensemble forecasts into the scenario generation process could improve the richness and accuracy of uncertainty representation in intraday price paths.

**Why It Matters:** External weather data is a primary driver of renewable energy production and thus electricity price dynamics; integrating it could significantly enhance the predictive power and realism of generated scenarios.

**Evidence:** the scenario generation approach is deliberately restricted to standard data sources and does not incorporate external meteorological ensemble forecasts.
