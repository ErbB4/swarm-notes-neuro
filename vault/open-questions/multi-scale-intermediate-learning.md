title: Learning Intermediate Time Scales
---

**Background:** Multi-scale time series forecasting models frequently utilize a binary division between global and local scales, failing to account for potentially important intermediate temporal patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Architectures need to be designed to accommodate multiple intermediate time scales beyond the standard global/local dichotomy while maintaining computational efficiency and a low memory footprint.

**Why It Matters:** Capturing intermediate scales is essential for extremely long sequences where significant temporal variations occur between the extremes of global and local contexts.
