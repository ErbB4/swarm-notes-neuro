title: Adaptive Period Discovery in Forecasting
---

**Background:** Time series forecasting often relies on autocorrelation-based analysis to determine inherent periodic intervals for downsampling or structural decomposition.

**Question / Future Work:** Current downsampling-based models remain highly sensitive to manually tuned or static period configurations, hindering adaptability to non-stationary or complex, multi-periodic signals. Developing mechanisms that can dynamically discover and adaptively select optimal periodic intervals during inference or training remains a critical bottleneck for robust long-term forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** It addresses a significant limitation in the robustness and automation of current state-of-the-art time series models that rely on periodic alignment.
