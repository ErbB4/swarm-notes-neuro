title: Joint estimation of valuation and patience
---

**Background:** Naor’s model assumes customers are homogeneous in their sensitivity to waiting costs, but in reality, customers may exhibit significant heterogeneity in both service valuation and delay sensitivity.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should explore the joint estimation of both service value and waiting cost sensitivity distributions in systems where balking is unobserved, extending the existing framework to more complex customer behaviors.

**Why It Matters:** Relaxing the homogeneity of delay sensitivity is critical for accurately modeling real-world queuing systems where customers have diverse time constraints.
