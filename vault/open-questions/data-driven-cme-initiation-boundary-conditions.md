title: Data-Driven CME Initiation Modeling
---

**Background:** Data-driven magnetohydrodynamic simulations currently rely on idealized boundary conditions or simplified flux-emergence procedures to initiate eruptive events.

**Question / Future Work:** Future modeling efforts aim to drive active region evolution using observational data, such as high-resolution vector magnetograms, to create self-consistent, data-driven simulations of the initiation, evolution, and propagation of coronal mass ejections.

**Why It Matters:** Data-driven boundary conditions are essential for moving from generic mechanism studies to predictive, real-world space weather forecasting based on actual observed solar active regions.
