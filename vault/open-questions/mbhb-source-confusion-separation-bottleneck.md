title: MBHB Source Confusion Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Massive black hole binary (MBHB) signals in the millihertz band often overlap in data streams, requiring robust methods for source separation beyond current single-source inference pipelines.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating whether normalizing flow architectures can be extended to perform concurrent multi-source separation and parameter estimation when signals overlap in the time-frequency domain.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing source confusion is a foundational requirement for the viability of future space-borne detectors like LISA.
