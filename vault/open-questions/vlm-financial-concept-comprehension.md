title: Evaluating VLM Financial Conceptual Understanding
---

**Background:** Vision-language models (VLMs) have shown potential in financial forecasting by analyzing candlestick charts, but their internal comprehension of specific technical indicators remains opaque.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop evaluation tasks that assess VLMs' explicit understanding of fundamental financial concepts such as resistance levels, support levels, and specific chart patterns, rather than relying solely on aggregate metrics like confusion matrices and information coefficients.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond aggregate performance metrics is essential to verify if models are truly performing technical analysis based on established financial theory or simply exploiting superficial statistical correlations.
