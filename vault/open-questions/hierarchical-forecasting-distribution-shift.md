title: Hierarchical Forecasting Distribution Shift
---

**Background:** Hierarchical video prediction frameworks decouple semantic reasoning from visual synthesis to improve temporal consistency, yet they suffer from train-test distribution shifts due to errors in autoregressive feature prediction.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust strategies to bridge the gap between ground-truth training representations and autoregressive, potentially noisy, predicted representations remains a bottleneck in ensuring stable, high-fidelity long-horizon video generation.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the error accumulation and distributional mismatch between stages is critical for the practical viability of multi-stage generative models.
