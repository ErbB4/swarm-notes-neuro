title: Adaptive Wavelet Decomposition Scaling
---

**Background:** Multi-scale signal decomposition via wavelets provides hierarchical temporal information, yet the optimal configuration of these scales remains sensitive to specific signal characteristics.

**Question / Future Work:** The effectiveness of multi-scale wavelet decomposition is observed to vary significantly across different time series domains, with some datasets benefiting from hierarchical analysis while others show negligible or negative impacts. Determining a principled, automated, or adaptive approach to selecting the number of decomposition levels for arbitrary time series inputs remains an unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the optimal wavelet decomposition depth dynamically would enable more robust and generalized architectures that adapt their hierarchical analysis to the specific complexity of input signals.
