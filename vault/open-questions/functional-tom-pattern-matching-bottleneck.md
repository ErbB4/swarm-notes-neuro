title: Isolating Functional ToM Reasoning
---

**Background:** Theory of Mind (ToM) benchmarks for LLMs often struggle to distinguish between genuine functional reasoning and the exploitation of lexical shortcuts or spurious correlations in conversation history.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open challenge to develop benchmarks that effectively isolate functional ToM from surface-level linguistic pattern matching, specifically by testing model performance on state-consistent trajectory prediction in the absence of informative context.

**Why It Matters:** This distinguishes between predictive linguistic pattern matching and actual agentic reasoning, a requirement for reliable social intelligence in future models.
