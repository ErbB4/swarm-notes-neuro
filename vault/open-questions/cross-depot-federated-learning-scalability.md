title: Cross-Depot Federated Learning Scalability
---

**Background:** Federated Learning (FL) models are increasingly evaluated for distributed EV charging infrastructure analytics where data heterogeneity exists across charging stations. Extending these frameworks beyond single-site deployments to collaborative multi-site environments is a critical step for scalability.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to transition from intra-depot federated learning (where stations at a single site act as clients) to cross-depot federated learning, which involves training models across multiple geographically or operationally distinct charging sites to improve model robustness and scalability.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling FL from a single depot to multiple depots introduces significant challenges related to data distribution shifts, varying connectivity, and communication efficiency across heterogeneous site-level infrastructures.
