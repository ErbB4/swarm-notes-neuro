title: Guidance for Complex Observations
---

**Background:** Diffusion-based data assimilation relies on likelihood-guidance mechanisms typically tailored to specific observation operators. Extending these frameworks to general, non-grid, or indirect measurement processes remains an open challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to extend inference-time guidance mechanisms beyond mask-type on-grid sensors to handle arbitrary, off-grid, and integrated observations, while addressing calibration and model error in the likelihood.

**Why It Matters:** It is a critical bottleneck for deploying diffusion-based data assimilation in real-world geophysical and climate settings where sensors are irregularly spaced or indirect.
