title: Multimodal Physiological Fusion Analysis
---

**Background:** Multimodal physiological monitoring integrates data from various sensors and signal sources to provide a more comprehensive assessment of health conditions. Combining diverse inputs such as pressure, electromyography, or imaging can overcome the limitations of relying on single-modality sensors.

**Question / Future Work:** Expanding low-latency streaming architectures to integrate and fuse heterogeneous physiological data streams (e.g., pressure plus EMG) presents challenges in balancing contextual richness with severe computational constraints for embedded deployment.

**Why It Matters:** Single-modality monitoring is often insufficient to fully distinguish overlapping physiological states; effective multimodal fusion is critical for reliable real-time diagnostic and closed-loop control systems.
