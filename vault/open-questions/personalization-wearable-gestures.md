title: Personalization in Wearable Gestures
---

**Background:** Smartwatch-based gesture recognition often faces challenges with individual user differences and the long-term consistency of gesture performance.

**Question / Future Work:** While subject-independent evaluation provides a baseline, developing efficient personalization or user-adaptation techniques under strict privacy constraints remains a significant hurdle for practical deployment in wearable systems.

**Why It Matters:** Personalization is essential for balancing system-wide accuracy with user-specific gesture nuances, impacting long-term model robustness and user satisfaction.
