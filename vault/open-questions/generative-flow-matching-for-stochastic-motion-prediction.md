title: Generative Flow Matching for Motion Prediction
---

**Background:** Stochastic motion prediction models often rely on injected Gaussian noise for Monte Carlo sampling, which serves as a surrogate for true probability distribution modeling.

**Question / Future Work:** Replacing manually injected stochastic perturbations in motion forecasting with generative models, such as Flow Matching, could enable the direct learning of continuous probability density vector fields, allowing for more mathematically rigorous modeling of multi-modal human intent.

**Why It Matters:** This shift from surrogate noise to learned generative dynamics is essential for improving the accuracy and theoretical grounding of uncertainty estimation in complex human-robot interaction scenarios.
