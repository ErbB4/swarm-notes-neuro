title: Fuel Cell EOL Definition
---

**Background:** Fuel cell (FC) degradation in maritime hybrid power systems is typically modeled using fixed assumptions about end-of-life (EOL) performance degradation thresholds.

**Question / Future Work:** The validity of the commonly assumed 10% performance drop as the criterion for FC end-of-life needs further evaluation. Future work should explore treating this threshold as an optimization variable rather than a fixed parameter to better reflect operational reality and economic objectives.

**Why It Matters:** The EOL definition significantly impacts the cost model and maintenance scheduling; using a fixed threshold may lead to suboptimal lifetime management.
