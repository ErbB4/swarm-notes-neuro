title: LLM structured data generalization bottleneck
---

**Background:** Foundation models trained on natural language often struggle with structured, non-linguistic domains due to the lack of explicit domain-specific inductive biases. Extending LLM-based sequence processing to diverse, heterogeneous structured data environments remains a significant challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate whether the proposed spatio-temporal steering paradigm can generalize to other non-linguistic, structured data structures and topologies beyond traffic network graphs to establish a more universal foundation model framework for time series.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental limitation of applying LLMs to structured domains, moving beyond task-specific models towards more adaptable foundation architectures.
