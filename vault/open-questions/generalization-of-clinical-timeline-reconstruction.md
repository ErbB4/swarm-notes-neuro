title: Generalization of Clinical Timeline Reconstruction
---

**Background:** Clinical timeline reconstruction faces a fundamental trade-off between the narrative richness of unstructured text and the temporal precision of structured EHR records.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating whether multimodal timeline alignment frameworks can maintain fidelity across non-sepsis domains, varied documentation styles, or settings with significant missing structured evidence remains an unresolved, critical bottleneck for clinical foundation models.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling beyond constrained, sepsis-specific benchmarks is essential to validate whether the proposed multimodal scaffold is a robust solution for heterogeneous patient trajectories.

**Evidence:** It will also be important to study generalization beyond sepsis... Another key question is how the framework should adapt when multimodal data is unavailable.
