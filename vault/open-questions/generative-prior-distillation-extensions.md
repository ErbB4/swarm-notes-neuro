title: Generative Prior Distillation Extensions
---

**Background:** Knowledge distillation often struggles when teacher and student models operate on sequences of vastly different lengths or completeness levels, leading to a target signal mismatch.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining effective distillation objectives that leverage generative priors for tasks beyond classification, such as time-series forecasting or multimodal learning with missing observations, remains a key research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental limitation of cross-context alignment in distillation, which is crucial for transfer learning in various sequential domains.
