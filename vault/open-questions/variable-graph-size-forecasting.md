title: Variable Graph Size Forecasting

**Background:** Network traffic exhibits dynamic, non-static structural evolution that is often constrained by fixed-size graph representations in current forecasting models.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop graph-based forecasting architectures capable of supporting variable graph sizes to better accommodate the non-static, evolving nature of real-world network traffic.

**Why It Matters:** Fixed-size constraints limit the scalability and realism of graph-based network traffic forecasting, acting as a critical bottleneck for production-grade deployment.

**Evidence:** Our future work will focus on ... supporting variable graph sizes, which is necessary to better model the non-static nature of network traffic.
