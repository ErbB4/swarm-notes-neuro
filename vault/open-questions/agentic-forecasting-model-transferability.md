title: Agentic Forecasting Model Transferability
---

**Background:** LLM agent systems for complex reasoning tasks often exhibit performance variances across base models.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open question whether the effectiveness of agentic frameworks—specifically structured belief state maintenance and hierarchical probability calibration—is robust to different underlying LLM architectures or model scales.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding model-agnosticism is critical for determining the scalability and robustness of the agentic forecasting framework across different computational environments.
