title: Stochastic Liquid Attention Networks
---

**Background:** In the context of continuous-time learning, integrating stochastic elements into ODE-based architectures allows for the modeling of inherent uncertainty in dynamical systems.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the replacement of the current deterministic Liquid Attention Network (LAN) ODE formulation with stochastic differential equations (SDEs) to enable intrinsic uncertainty quantification within the attention mechanism.

**Why It Matters:** This transition could significantly improve the robustness and reliability of the model in real-world applications where data is noisy or stochastic in nature.

**Evidence:** Replacing the LAN-ODE with stochastic differential equation (SDE) is a natural direction for future work, as it will introduce intrinsic uncertainty quantification into the attention mechanism.
