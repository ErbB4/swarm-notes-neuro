title: Long-horizon Tactical Coherence
---

**Background:** While diffusion models have demonstrated capabilities in generating realistic player trajectories in sports, their ability to maintain long-horizon coordination across complex multi-agent interactions remains a challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Current generative frameworks for football tactics often struggle to maintain long-term tactical coherence when generating sequences, as fixed-horizon sampling can lead to a loss of team-level structure over time. Research is needed to develop architectures or sampling strategies that ensure cohesive multi-agent interaction over extended durations.

**Why It Matters:** Long-horizon tactical consistency is the primary bottleneck for transitioning from realistic trajectory prediction to useful decision-support tools for professional tactical planning.
