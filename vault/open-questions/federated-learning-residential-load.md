title: Federated Learning for Residential Forecasting
---

**Background:** Smart meter data is highly sensitive and decentralized, presenting significant privacy barriers to the development of large-scale, robust forecasting models. Federated learning is a proposed paradigm for training on such data without centralization.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should formalize the application of federated learning to train global temporal forecasting models across heterogeneous residential households, balancing the need for model performance with strict data privacy requirements.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses the scalability-privacy trade-off inherent in residential smart grid management.
