title: Flexible Multi-Agent Trajectory Modeling
---

**Background:** Multi-modal generative models for trajectory forecasting and completion often operate on fixed temporal horizons, which limits their adaptability to diverse real-world task settings and scenarios.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing multi-agent trajectory modeling architectures that are not constrained by fixed temporal window dimensions or a predefined number of agents remains a challenging open problem for ensuring broader applicability.

**Why It Matters:** Flexibility in temporal and spatial dimensions is critical for applying trajectory modeling to varying, real-world sports and surveillance scenarios where observation durations and agent counts change dynamically.
