title: Operational Digital Twin Integration
---

**Background:** AI-driven digital twins for flood forecasting require the integration of surrogate models with real-time observational data to maintain accuracy under changing environmental conditions. Current surrogates often operate on static assumptions or limited observational feedback.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved need for an end-to-end framework that effectively integrates hydrodynamic surrogates with real-time observation assimilation, enabling the model to dynamically update its state based on sparse, high-fidelity gauge measurements or remote sensing. Developing closed-loop data assimilation for latent neural surrogates is critical for practical flood monitoring and decision support systems.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between surrogate inference and real-time state estimation is necessary to transform surrogate models into functional, operational digital twins.
