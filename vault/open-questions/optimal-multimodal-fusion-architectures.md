title: Optimal Multimodal Fusion Architectures
---

**Background:** Multimodal remote sensing datasets integrate heterogeneous data sources, yet designing architectures that optimally fuse these disparate modalities remains an unsolved challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** While various fusion strategies exist, there is no consensus on how to best align and integrate spatial, spectral, and temporal features from sensors with significantly different resolutions and properties. Further research is required to determine the ideal parameter allocation and fusion mechanisms to prevent performance degradation when integrating diverse Earth Observation data streams.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding optimal fusion mechanisms is critical for maximizing the utility of increasingly complex and multimodal Earth Observation datasets.
