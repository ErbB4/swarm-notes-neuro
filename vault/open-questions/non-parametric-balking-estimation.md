title: Non-parametric estimation for unobserved balking
---

**Background:** Queuing systems with heterogeneous customer service valuations and unobserved balking behavior are typically analyzed using parametric statistical models. However, the exact parametric form of the customer service-value distribution is often unknown in real-world applications.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work could investigate whether non-parametric or semi-parametric estimation techniques can relax the need for assuming a specific parametric form for the service-value distribution, while maintaining interpretability.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the restrictive nature of parametric assumptions in strategic queuing models, potentially expanding applicability to real-world scenarios where underlying utility distributions are unknown.
