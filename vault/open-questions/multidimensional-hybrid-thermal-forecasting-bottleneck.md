title: Multidimensional hybrid thermal forecasting
---

**Background:** Physics-informed machine learning combines data-driven neural networks with physical laws expressed through regularization terms in the loss function to improve predictive accuracy and physical consistency.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is required to extend physics-informed forecasting architectures to multidimensional domains and deep electrochemical-thermal coupling, which remain critical for high-fidelity safety modeling in complex battery pack geometries.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond 1D thermal constraints to multi-physics coupling is a non-trivial challenge for deploying robust, interpretable forecasting models in physical infrastructure.
