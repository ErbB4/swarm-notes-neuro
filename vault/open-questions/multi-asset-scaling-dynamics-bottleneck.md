title: Multi-Asset Scaling Dynamics Bottleneck
---

**Background:** High-dimensional, cross-sectional, and synchronous financial data contains structured dependencies that single-asset models fail to exploit. Neural-parameterized operator models currently exist only at a single-asset level of complexity.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to scale neural-parameterized Markov operator frameworks to high-frequency, synchronous data across a multi-asset universe. The challenge lies in expanding the state space to a portfolio-level operator while leveraging cross-sectional correlation dynamics without encountering the curse of dimensionality.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for the practical utility of the framework in systematic risk management and for overcoming signal-to-noise limitations inherent in single-asset modeling.

**Evidence:** Transitioning from a single-asset state space to a portfolio-level operator is expected to materially improve predictive accuracy by leveraging cross-sectional correlation dynamics.
