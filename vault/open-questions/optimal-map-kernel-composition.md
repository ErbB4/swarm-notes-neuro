title: Optimal Map-Kernel Composition
---

**Background:** The effectiveness of mapping techniques in reproducing kernel Hilbert space (RKHS) methods is highly sensitive to the specific choice of kernel, yet a systematic understanding of this interplay remains limited.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal composition of scaling maps and kernels for specific data domains remains largely empirical, posing a barrier to automated kernel design.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental bottleneck for automating kernel engineering and optimizing performance across diverse tasks without exhaustive trial-and-error.

**Evidence:** It is crucial to observe that the effectiveness of a specific map can differ significantly depending upon the choice of kernel.
