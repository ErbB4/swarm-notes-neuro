title: Physics-constrained Assimilation Integration
---

**Background:** Scientific dynamical systems often exhibit complex, non-linear behaviors governed by underlying physical laws, yet many current data assimilation frameworks rely primarily on data-driven trajectory priors.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to integrate physical constraints and reward-driven fine-tuning into learned trajectory priors to ensure state estimates remain consistent with conservation laws (e.g., mass, energy) and improve long-horizon stability.

**Why It Matters:** Integrating physics constraints is crucial for ensuring the physical realism and reliability of assimilated state estimates in scientific and climate applications.
