title: Exogenous Integration in Dynamic Forecasting
---

**Background:** Deep time series forecasting models often rely primarily on endogenous signal features, which may limit their capacity to handle external events or system-wide shocks.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work needs to investigate how dynamic recalibration mechanisms can incorporate multimodal exogenous covariates to move beyond pure autoregressive modeling and achieve more robust, anticipatory forecasting in real-world systems.

**Why It Matters:** Exogenous integration is a critical bottleneck for deploying forecasting models in volatile, real-world environments where external data streams significantly influence time series outcomes.
