title: Geographic and Climatic Generalization
---

**Background:** Transformer models rely on attention mechanisms to capture temporal dependencies, but their ability to generalize across geographically and climatically diverse regions remains largely unverified.

**Question / Future Work:** The current evaluation of transformer-based solar forecasting models is largely limited to specific regional datasets. There is a need to validate these models on broader, geographically diverse, and climatically variant datasets—particularly those encompassing extreme weather conditions or unforeseen events—to assess their true generalization capabilities and robustness.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is the primary challenge in scaling solar forecasting solutions to global power grids, where weather patterns and PV system configurations vary significantly.
