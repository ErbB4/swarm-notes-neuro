title: Real-time infrastructure deployment scalability

**Background:** Computational anomaly detection methods often face a trade-off between model accuracy and real-time execution constraints in critical infrastructure monitoring. Evaluating this trade-off is essential for deployment in systems where response time is critical.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating how advanced structural anomaly detection models perform under hardware-constrained, real-time deployment conditions remains an unresolved bottleneck for practical adoption in critical infrastructure.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for transitioning from high-dimensional latent modeling to reliable, time-sensitive operation in real-world critical infrastructure.
