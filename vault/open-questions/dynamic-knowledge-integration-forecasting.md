title: Dynamic Knowledge Integration
---

**Background:** Large language models are increasingly used as semantic priors in time-series forecasting, yet the translation of numerical data into semantic prompts often relies on static, rule-based templates.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining effective methods for integrating dynamic, unstructured, and heterogeneous external knowledge (e.g., news, event logs) directly into forecasting frameworks, moving beyond the current reliance on rule-based prompting templates.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental barrier to evolving LLM-based time series forecasting from static statistical assistance to dynamic, reasoning-driven adaptive systems.
