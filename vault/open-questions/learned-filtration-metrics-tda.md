title: Learned Filtration Metrics for TDA
---

**Background:** Topological data analysis (TDA) typically relies on pre-defined filtration metrics (like Pearson distance) to characterize the structure of data populations, which may not capture complex non-linear dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a learnable filtration metric for TDA could allow for more robust recovery of latent topological structures in time-series populations, potentially overcoming limitations imposed by standard distance metrics in forecasting contexts.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond fixed distance metrics is a significant hurdle in making TDA more effective for high-dimensional, non-linear forecasting data.
