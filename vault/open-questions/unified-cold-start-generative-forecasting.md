title: Unified Cold-Start Forecasting Frameworks
---

**Background:** Conditional generative forecasting frameworks often lack the ability to adapt to new, product-specific information as it becomes available after launch.

**Question / Future Work:** An open question remains in constructing a unified, cold-start forecasting framework that simultaneously handles pre-launch information and sequentially adapts to post-launch observed prefixes within a single probabilistic model. Specifically, future work should address how to better integrate diverse contextual signals and interventions, such as marketing actions, search trends, and platform-mediated exposure dynamics, into the latent state representation of such generative models.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for improving the practical utility of generative forecasting in operational settings where product-specific data is sparse and dynamic information evolves over time.
