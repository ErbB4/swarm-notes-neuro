title: Recurrence analysis for MBL
---

**Background:** Quantum many-body systems can exhibit ergodicity breaking through phenomena such as many-body localization, which is typically identified by examining the scaling and transport properties of disordered systems.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains to be determined whether recurrence plot analysis can effectively distinguish ergodicity-breaking phases, such as many-body localization, by characterizing the behavior of correlation functions across varying disorder strengths. Investigating the efficacy of these non-linear time-series tools as a diagnostic for localization-delocalization transitions represents a potentially significant expansion of the current framework.

**Why It Matters:** Determining whether recurrence quantification can identify critical disorder strengths without extensive system-size scaling would provide a computationally efficient, unsupervised diagnostic for ergodicity breaking in quantum dynamics.

**Evidence:** In presence of a sufficiently strong disorder some systems break ergodicity through many-body localization [6, 7, 8]. RP analysis of the correlation functions across different disorder strengths could supplement current studies [76, 9] on determining the critical disorder strength for ergodicity breaking.
