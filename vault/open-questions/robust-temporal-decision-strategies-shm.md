title: Robust temporal decision strategies for SHM
---

**Background:** Structural damage identification in practice often relies on analyzing vibration signal windows, but individual window-level classification may be unreliable due to transient noise or non-stationary operational conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should develop robust decision-making strategies, such as temporal aggregation or statistical hypothesis testing across signal sequences, to reduce false positives and increase confidence in damage diagnosis compared to isolated window-level inference.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental reliability gap between local model inference and global structural state assessment in SHM.
