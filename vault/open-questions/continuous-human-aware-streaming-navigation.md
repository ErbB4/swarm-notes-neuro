title: Continuous Human-Aware Navigation Streaming
---

**Background:** Vision-Language Navigation (VLN) agents often operate in environments with dynamic human movement, yet current frameworks largely rely on intermittent, stop-and-wait approaches to process human dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** The extension of human-centric navigation from episodic stop-and-wait interactions to continuous, real-time streaming navigation in dynamic environments remains an unresolved challenge. Existing models typically process human information during brief pauses, failing to account for the complexities of seamless, ongoing interactions where humans and robots move simultaneously.

**Why It Matters:** Transitioning to a continuous streaming setting is essential for practical deployment in realistic human-populated settings, where stop-and-wait behaviors are often impractical or socially disruptive.

**Evidence:** Future work will focus on extending the current stop-and-wait design to a continuous streaming setting...
