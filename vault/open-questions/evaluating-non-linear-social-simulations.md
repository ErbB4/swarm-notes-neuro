title: Evaluating Non-Linear Social Simulations
---

**Background:** The validation of simulations with complex, non-linear, or branching temporal dynamics remains a significant challenge for existing process-based evaluation frameworks.

**Question / Future Work:** Current evaluation methods often rely on monotonic temporal alignment (e.g., Dynamic Time Warping), which is insufficient for capturing simulations that exhibit non-linear social time, branching scenarios, or looping topological structures. Future research is needed to develop metrics capable of assessing structural realism in simulations where the order and progression of social events do not conform to a simple, linear timeline relative to empirical ground truth.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical limitation for the scalability and applicability of process-based validation frameworks to diverse social phenomena where non-linearity is a core feature.
