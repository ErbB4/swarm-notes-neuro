title: Target-Side Information Decomposition Bottleneck
---

**Background:** While source-side information redundancy in multivariate systems can be addressed through interventions, a formal, symmetric framework for decomposing information on the target-variable side is currently lacking.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a formal partition of target-side informational atoms to characterize redundancy and synergy in one-to-many causal mappings is required to complete the information decomposition framework.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for providing a fully symmetric causal analysis of source-target interactions, which is critical for complex system modelling.
