title: Robust Predictive Risk Assessment
---

**Background:** In dynamic UAV-assisted edge networks, successor reservation strategies rely on predictive models to initiate resource allocation before service disruptions occur. These proactive frameworks typically function under specific assumptions about environmental stationarity and network topology dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** Predictive models for service disruption are sensitive to non-stationary environments and long-term dynamical changes. Establishing the limits of predictive robustness in these volatile scenarios is critical for scaling proactive edge-service reservation.

**Why It Matters:** Predictive accuracy is the bottleneck for the entire proactive reservation process; if predictions are inaccurate, the system incurs unnecessary reservation overhead or fails to prepare for actual failures.
