title: Automated PDRS Parameter Selection
---

**Background:** Peak-Driven Region Segmentation (PDRS) relies on user-defined free parameters to define the thresholds for segment identification and merging.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing adaptive or automated procedures to select hyperparameters for PDRS to ensure consistent segmentation performance across varied, high-volume time-series datasets without human intervention.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for enabling fully autonomous data-processing pipelines in large-scale surveys where manual tuning is not feasible.

**Evidence:** A trade-off of the PDRS framework is its reliance on a set of user-defined free parameters.
