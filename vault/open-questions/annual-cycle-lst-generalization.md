title: Year-round LST model generalization
---

**Background:** Land Surface Temperature (LST) estimation models often rely on warm-season data, but seasonal variability in surface energy balance and environmental drivers may limit their year-round effectiveness.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating how LST downscaling and nowcasting models can be extended to perform accurately throughout the full annual cycle, including cold-season conditions where physical drivers differ, remains an unresolved challenge. The authors suggest that this may require either developing season-specific models or incorporating additional predictors to better capture year-round landscape and environmental controls.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization across seasons is critical for the practical, continuous operational use of satellite-based LST monitoring for urban heat and climate applications.
