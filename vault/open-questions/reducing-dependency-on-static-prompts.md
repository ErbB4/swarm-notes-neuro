title: Mitigating static-data dependency
---

**Background:** The precision of physiological signal generation is often heavily dependent on static participant-specific metadata, which may be unavailable in emergency or data-scarce clinical environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should investigate methods to reduce dependency on patient-specific static conditioning prompts, allowing foundation models to maintain personalized synthesis performance even when demographic or clinical metadata is missing.

**Why It Matters:** Reducing this dependency is essential for deploying these models in real-world, unpredictable clinical settings where comprehensive patient data is not always accessible.
