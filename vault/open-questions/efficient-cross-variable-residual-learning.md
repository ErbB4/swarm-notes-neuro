title: Efficient Cross-Variable Residual Learning
---

**Background:** Long-term multivariate time series forecasting faces challenges in capturing inter-variable dependencies efficiently without introducing significant parameter redundancy or optimization instability.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need for stable, well-constrained cross-variable residual modules that can capture complex inter-dependencies in high-dimensional datasets while maintaining the generalization advantages of simpler, channel-independent models.

**Why It Matters:** This is central to advancing multivariate forecasting beyond channel-independent paradigms.

**Evidence:** More efficient and stable cross-variable residual modules may better adapt to high-dimensional multivariate datasets such as Traffic and Electricity.
