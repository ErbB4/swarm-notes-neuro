title: Federated Learning for Agricultural Privacy
---

**Background:** In agricultural crop yield prediction systems, maintaining both high predictive accuracy and the privacy of localized, sensitive agricultural data across distributed geographic regions remains a significant challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop federated learning-based architectures for spatio-temporal crop yield prediction. This approach would enable the training of robust global models on regional, decentralized datasets without necessitating the sharing of sensitive, localized raw data, thereby balancing performance with data privacy and regulatory compliance.

**Why It Matters:** This is technically important as agricultural yield data is often proprietary or subject to strict privacy regulations, and traditional centralized training methods are becoming increasingly untenable for large-scale, cross-regional model deployment.
