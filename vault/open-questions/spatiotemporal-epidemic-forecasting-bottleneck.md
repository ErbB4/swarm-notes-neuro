title: Spatiotemporal Epidemic Forecasting Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Epidemic forecasting models often struggle to incorporate explicit spatial interactions, typically treating data as multivariate time series without accounting for geographical propagation.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to evolve current epidemic forecasting frameworks from multivariate temporal modeling toward explicit spatiotemporal modeling to capture disease spread dynamics across connected regions.

**Why It Matters:** Spatial spread is the fundamental nature of epidemics; current multivariate benchmarks remain limited by their focus on temporal correlations over spatial mechanisms.

**Evidence:** EpiCastBench is designed for multivariate forecasting, it does not explicitly capture spatial dependencies and can be extended to spatiotemporal settings.
