title: Optimal Neural Manifold Discovery
---

**Background:** Neural activity is known to be constrained to low-dimensional manifolds, yet most foundation models continue to utilize Euclidean space assumptions.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to determine how to automatically learn or adapt the intrinsic Riemannian manifold structure of data rather than relying on manual, predefined manifold selections during architecture design.

**Why It Matters:** The geometric structure significantly influences model performance, and transitioning from manual selection to automated/learned manifold discovery is critical for model scalability and robustness.

**Evidence:** The current framework requires the manual selection of specific Riemannian manifolds (e.g., hypersphere or hyperbolic).
