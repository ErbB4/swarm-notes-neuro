title: Modeling Latent Neuromodulatory Dynamics
---

**Background:** The task of mapping high-dimensional neural activity to behavioral outputs is complicated by the presence of unobserved latent variables, such as neuromodulators, which influence motor dynamics but are often missing from experimental datasets.

**Question / Future Work:** A key open challenge is extending current neuromechanical modeling frameworks to account for latent state variables (e.g., hormones, neurotransmitters) that are essential for accurate behavioral prediction but are not directly measurable in standard recording sessions.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding these latent influences is a fundamental bottleneck to achieving reliable predictive control in closed-loop biological systems.
