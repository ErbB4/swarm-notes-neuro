title: Heavy-tailed HMM for operational risk
---

**Background:** Hidden Markov Models (HMMs) typically assume Gaussian emission distributions for analytical tractability, which can fail to adequately capture the heavy-tailed nature of financial operational losses.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to extend current HMM frameworks for operational risk to utilize heavy-tailed emission distributions, such as the Student-t distribution or other elliptical families, to better model tail risk and extreme loss events.

**Why It Matters:** This extension is critical for financial risk management where the underestimation of extreme losses (tail risk) leads to significant capital inadequacy.
