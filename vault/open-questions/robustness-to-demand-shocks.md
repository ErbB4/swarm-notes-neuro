title: Robustness to Demand Shocks
---

**Background:** Hybrid predictive-prescriptive supply chain frameworks often struggle to maintain decision quality under conditions of non-stationary, extreme demand volatility and unpredictable supply-side constraints.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to improve model robustness during consecutive, rapid demand shocks where forecasting modules frequently under-predict peaks, and to develop adaptive constraints that manage scenarios where supplier lead times exceed static model assumptions.

**Why It Matters:** Improving resilience against extreme demand volatility is a fundamental barrier to the deployment of integrated predictive-prescriptive systems in real-world industrial environments.
