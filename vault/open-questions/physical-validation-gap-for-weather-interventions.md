title: NWP Validation of Interventions
---

**Background:** Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) models and data-driven diffusion forecasting models often operate under different dynamics, leading to a physical validation gap.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a critical need to bridge the discrepancy between data-driven weather interventions and NWP systems, where perturbations must be dynamically balanced to survive forecast integration rather than decaying instantly due to instability.

**Why It Matters:** Without validation against physical systems, data-driven interventions risk being artifacts of the surrogate model rather than physically feasible modifications.

**Evidence:** Perturbations that are not dynamically balanced may rapidly decay during forecast integration.
