title: Adaptive Covariate Selection Under Dependence
---

**Background:** The selection of an optimal regressor index in high-dimensional marginal regression settings often necessitates adaptive post-model-selection inference.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of an endogenously selected optimal covariate index remains to be explored under weak dependence. This is particularly relevant for constructing tests that avoid the limitations of current pre-estimation screening methods while maintaining robust size and power properties.

**Why It Matters:** Developing such an index would bridge the gap between simple marginal screening and complex post-model-selection inference, potentially enhancing the detection of informative predictors in dependent data.
