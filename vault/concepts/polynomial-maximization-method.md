# Polynomial Maximization Method (PMM)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A parameter estimation framework for regression and time-series models that utilizes higher-order cumulants to achieve efficiency gains over OLS in the presence of asymmetric or leptokurtic errors.


## Why It Matters

PMM provides a robust parameter estimation framework for non-Gaussian scenarios by leveraging higher-order moments, offering a distinct alternative to standard OLS.

## Evidence

> PMM exploits higher-order cumulants of the error distribution -- specifically the third standardized moment gamma_3 and fourth standardized moment gamma_4 -- to construct estimators that outperform ordinary least squares (OLS) whenever the errors are asymmetric or leptokurtic.
