# Contrastive Dimension Reduction

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A dimensionality reduction paradigm that extracts signals unique to a foreground group relative to a background control group.


## Why It Matters

It is the central topic of the paper and represents a distinct family of dimensionality reduction techniques that contrast datasets rather than just decomposing one.
